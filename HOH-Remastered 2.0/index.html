Our Mission

To provide guidance, care, prevention and comprehensive assistance to women (and their children) who find themselves in abusive households. We wish to foster nonviolence and gender equality.

Counseling

Professionals in the fields of mental, physical, and spiritual health will be available to help. With much love, understanding, we hope to teach victims of domestic violence to love themselves so they can love their children and other people and stop feeling guilty and to erase the notion that they brought their abuse on themselves.

Social Support

Our staff and volunteers will be available to provide advice and personal guidance on issues related to domestic violence. We will also be available to help victims find emergency shelters and transitional homes if they are unable to return to their home for fear of violence or economic instability.
